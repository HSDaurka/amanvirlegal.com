Commercial Vehicle Operators

CVOR Violations & Truck Driver Offences

Your Commercial Vehicle Operator's Registration (CVOR) is the lifeline of your trucking career or business. A single violation can trigger MTO audits, safety rating deterioration, or complete licence cancellation. We understand the stakes.

Whether you are an owner-operator, fleet manager, or long-haul driver, we provide aggressive representation to protect your CVOR record and keep your business on the road.

  • Hours of Service (HOS) violations & logbook offences
  • Overweight & over-dimension infractions
  • Unsafe vehicle & equipment violations (brakes, lights, tires)
  • Failure to inspect / inspection certificate offences
  • Dangerous goods & load securement violations
  • MTO roadside inspection failures
  • CVOR point accumulation & facility audits
  • Speeding in a commercial vehicle
  • Following too closely (tailgating) in a CMV
  • Failure to stop at weigh scales
  • Driver's abstract & carrier profile reviews
  • CVOR licence reinstatement hearings
