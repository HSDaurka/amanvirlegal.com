Criminal Code Defence

Criminal & Serious Driving Offences

Criminal charges arising from driving incidents carry life-changing consequences including criminal records, licence suspension, and potential incarceration. Time is critical — contact us immediately.

🍺 Impaired Driving (DUI / Over 80)

Being charged with impaired driving or operating a vehicle with a blood alcohol concentration over 80mg is a serious Criminal Code offence. We challenge breathalyzer accuracy, officer conduct, Charter rights violations, and Approved Instrument calibration to build a rigorous defence.

🚫 Refusing a Breathalyzer

Refusal to provide a breath sample carries the same penalties as a DUI conviction. We analyse the lawfulness of the demand, your right to counsel, and procedural compliance to challenge the charge effectively.

💨 Drug-Impaired Driving

Cannabis and drug-impaired driving charges rely on Drug Recognition Expert (DRE) evaluations and oral fluid samples. We scrutinize evaluation procedures, chain of custody, and officer training to defend your rights.

💥 Dangerous Operation of a Vehicle

A Criminal Code offence far more serious than careless driving, dangerous operation charges require an aggressive defence examining the standard of driving, road conditions, and witness credibility.

🚑 Failure to Stop at an Accident

Leaving the scene of a collision involving injury or death is a Criminal Code offence. We assess the evidence, your awareness of the accident, and any applicable Charter defences to protect your rights.

⚠️ Criminal Negligence Causing Harm

Among the most serious driving-related Criminal Code charges, we provide comprehensive defence representation examining every aspect of the Crown's case including expert evidence and causation arguments.
